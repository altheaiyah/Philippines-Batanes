Batan Island: The Heart of Batanes

Home to the capital town of Basco, where every road leads to breathtaking views. Known for its rolling hills, lighthouses, and panoramic coastal views which capture the island's timeless beauty.

Vayang Rolling Hills

Overlooking the West Philippine Sea, Vayang Rolling Hills is a sea of endless greens and blues that perfectly captures the raw beauty of Batanes.

Basco Lighthouse

A scenic viewpoint atop Naidi Hills, offering a panoramic sight of Basco and the sea.

Valugan Boulder Beach

A rocky shore sculpted by centuries of waves.

Marlboro Country

Also known as Rakuh a Payaman, offering a vast view of hills meeting the sea.

House of Dakay

Recognized as a UNESCO Heritage Building and said to be the oldest stone house in Batanes.

Homoron Blue Lagoon

Calm turquoise waters and smooth stones that invite peaceful reflection.

Alapad Pass

A scenic mountain pass offering panoramic views of Batanes' rolling hills and the West Philippine Sea.

Sabtang Island: The Soul of Ivatan Heritage

A living showcase of Ivatan culture, known for its stone houses, rugged cliffs, and scenic coastal roads that preserve the timeless charm of Batanes.

Chamantad–Tinyan Viewpoint

A sweeping vista of rolling hills and one of the island’s most iconic viewpoints.

Savidug Stone Houses

Traditional limestone homes built to withstand both time and typhoons.

Morong Beach

Famous for the iconic natural rock formation, the Nakabuang Arch.

Sabtang Lighthouse

The lighthouse stands as a steadfast guide for islanders and travelers alike.

Sabtang Port

A vital gateway for trade and travel, showcasing the island’s maritime heritage.

Itbayat Island: The Edge of the North

The northernmost island of Batanes. A rugged frontier where towering cliffs, deep caves, and breathtaking landscapes showcase nature’s raw power and beauty.

Rapang Cliff

A dramatic formation standing tall over the northern seas.

Cavaywan Lake

A serene freshwater lake nestled amidst Itbayat's rugged terrain.

Chinapoliran Port

The island’s main gateway.
