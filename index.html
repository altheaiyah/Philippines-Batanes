A Glimpse of Batanes

Batanes forms the northernmost province of the Philippines. Consist of ten volcanic islands with only three are permanently inhabited: Batan (home to the provincial capital, Basco), Sabtang, and Itbayat. A fourth island, Ivuhos, which hosts a few families who raise cattle. The remaining islands—Yami, North, Mavudis, Siayan, Di-nem, and Dequey—are uninhabited. Administratively, the province consists of six municipalities: Ivana, Uyugan, Mahatao, and Basco on Batan Island, along with the island municipalities of Sabtang and Itbayat.
