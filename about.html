About VayanBatanes

A heartfelt project dedicated to showcasing the beauty, culture, and timeless charm of Batanes

VayanBatanes

The word “Vayan” comes from the Ivatan language, meaning “to go” or “to visit.” Combined with Batanes, VayanBatanes simply means “Go to Batanes” or "Visit Batanes." This project serves as an invitation to discover more about the province.

VayanBatanes was created to share the beauty, culture, and simple way of life in Batanes. It aims to let others appreciate the charm of the Ivatan people and their deep connection with nature.

Batanes has always been my dream local destination, and it holds a special place in my heart because of its peaceful landscapes, the simplicity of life, and how the Ivatans preserve their islands with care and resilience. Through this website, I hope to show others what makes Batanes truly special and worth cherishing. A reminder of how beautiful and inspiring our local destinations can be.
